How to Wipe Your Android Device Using Settings

  1. Open the Settings app on your Android device.
  2. Scroll down and select System.
  3. Tap on Reset options.
  4. Choose Erase all data (factory reset).
  5. Confirm by tapping Reset phone or Erase everything.
  6. Enter your PIN/password if prompted, and your device will begin wiping.

This process will erase all user data and restore your device to factory settings.
